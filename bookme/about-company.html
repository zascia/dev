    The plan for general cleaning of residential premises: apartments, cottage houses includes the following services: - dry and wet floor cleaning;
    - cleaning and washing dirty dishes;
    - disinfection plumbing;
    - washing glass surfaces, windows and mirrors;
    - removal of dirt and dust in all living rooms of the house, apartment;
    - removing limescale from the surface of tiles and sanitary ware;
    - elimination of blockages in sewer pipes;
    - cleaning dust from lighting and interior items;
    - taking out the trash;
    - washing the working surface of the stove;
