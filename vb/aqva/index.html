Our Method

We find water using our unique technology which combines scientific research, software and hardware tools. This is our know how that guarantees finding water in 100% of cases.

The water search process is performed in two stages:

1. Field stage. Probably the most interesting part of our research is conducting field discoveries. These discoveries aim to achieve particular practical results by using of our technology to find a water source.

2. Laboratory stage consists of a mathematical study of the measurement results recorded during on the field stage and calculating the precise location of the borehall and drilling depth.

Our Guarantee

The water discovery industry is known for its risks, which can be called a fear of failure. That's why companies will come up with a huge bill regardless of the result. At Aqva World, based on solid experience and in-depth expertise, we offer our customers the following model – you pay only if we find water.

Moreover, after the drilling phase, we guarantee discovered water source potential is no lower than 100,000 litre daily or more than 90% from originally forecasted capacity.

We also guarantee water source capacity. In the case that discovered source capacity decreases by more than 30% within 3 years after our services are carried out, we guarantee either to refresh drilling of the water borehall, or to find another new water drilling point in a 1 km area.

Working process

  • 1

    Preparation

    Signing agreement
    Calculating water demand
    Researching water logistics

  • 2

    Permits

    The customer obtains all necessary permits

  • 3

    Discovery

    Two dedicated onsite engineerst
    Defining 2 drilling pinpoints and depth
    The process takes 2 days

  • 4

    Drilling

    The Client provides required equipment.
    Drilling equipment capacity should be 300+ meters depth
    Drill diameter 300 mm,
    Air compressor technology
    The process takes 2 days

  • 5

    Audit

    Once water is detected, detection,
    capacity is measured using pump tool
    and compared to the original forecast

  • 6

    Furnishing

    The Client provides the furnishing based on our requirements
    The process usually takes 2-3 days
