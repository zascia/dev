Web development

You have an idea and some budget. Your next step is to find a team of developers who could implement it. Big companies look very experienced and stable. They had been proved by their status and long history and they has really worth prices. In that way, you should be prepared to deal with pretty high budget. Not your case?
Next step is visiting freelancer resources. Among the great variety of candidates you expect to find a right person for your project. Good idea! Having understanding that the cheapest may be not the best and the best is also worth its cost you'd need to find an optimal way. Very useful way is to get known more people around you and share your time for that before getting result, isn’t it?
There is another way to deal with small companies that has an expertise in issues that you need and appreciates a customer for what they are. Our goal is to satisfy customer needs covering as big variety of requirements as possible. We don't have a numerous in-house staff so don't take extra fees for that. Our work model is to create a team dedicated to special requirements of customer that works on a remote and distributed basis. It allows us to cover multiple areas of skills. Moreover, it allows us to maintain up to 20 hours of development process daily in case of need.  Sounds nice?
